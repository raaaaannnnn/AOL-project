About Us

FontDale was envisioned by Franz Diego in 2023. His vision was simple: to create a space where the elegance of traditional galleries meets the energy of a modern lifestyle hub.


Officially opened in 2025, the mall serves as a cornerstone of Arcadia Circle. What makes FontDale unique is the Lumen Skywalk—a signature architectural marvel connecting the North Gallery and South Pavilion, offering breathtaking views of the city.


Built with a commitment to quality and contemporary design, we prioritize sensory experiences, ensuring every visit feels like a journey.


This journey begins the moment you step through our doors. The North Gallery is designed as a sanctuary of curated luxury, where high-end fashion and artisanal boutiques are showcased with the quiet reverence of a museum. In contrast, the South Pavilion pulses with the kinetic energy of modern life, housing avant-garde tech spaces, interactive wellness studios, and a global culinary scene that caters to the city's boldest trendsetters.
